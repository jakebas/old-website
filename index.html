About Me

Currently, I work as Code.org's Director of District Management, where I lead an outreach team that has helped bring computer science to over 100 school districts nationwide, including the country's 7 largest. In this role I work closely with district leadership to craft a sustainable plan to bring computer science to students, with a focus on reaching traditionally underrepresented groups in computing. I have also worked with Code.org's curriculum writing team on early drafts of the CS Principles curriculum.

Prior to working with Code.org I was the Computer Science Department Chair at Lindblom Math and Science Academy in Chicago's West Englewood neighborhood, where I built a pathway of computer science courses and more than doubled enrollment by young women in computer science classes. I also led professional development for the Exploring Computer Science course for teachers across the country. Before entering the classroom I studied computer science at Swarthmore College, with a focus on distributed computing (and poetry).
